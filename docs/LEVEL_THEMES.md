# AI Job Wars - Level Themes and Design

This document details the narrative themes and gameplay design for each of the 12 levels.

## Level Design Philosophy

Each level represents a different challenge in the modern job search and workplace experience:
- Progressive difficulty through enemy density and speed
- Thematic coherence with real-world job market frustrations
- Visual and narrative storytelling through cutscenes
- Boss battles at tier transitions (L6, L10, L11, L12)

## Difficulty Scaling

### Enemy Health & Damage Multipliers
- **Tier 1** (L1-L3): 1.0x baseline
- **Tier 2** (L4-L6): 1.2x (+20%)
- **Tier 3** (L7-L9): 1.5x (+50%)
- **Tier 4** (L10-L11): 2.0x (+100%)
- **Final Boss** (L12): 2.5x (+150%)

### Scroll Speed Progression
- L1: Speed 4 (Tutorial)
- L2-L3: Speed 5 (Learning)
- L4-L5: Speed 6 (Moderate)
- L6: Speed 7 (Mini-boss)
- L7: Speed 7 (Hard)
- L8-L9: Speed 8 (Very Hard)
- L10-L11: Speed 9 (Boss tier)
- L12: Speed 10 (Maximum)

### Wave Counts (Enemy Spawns)
Progressively increasing from 3 (L1) to 15 (L12)

---

## Powerup Placement Strategy

- **Health (h)**: Placed in mid-tier levels (L4, L7, L10)
- **Shield (s)**: Placed in difficult levels (L5, L8, L11)
- **Weapon (W)**: Placed before boss encounters (L6, L9)

Strategic placement ensures players have resources for major challenges.

## Boss Battle Design

### Mini-Boss: Level 6 - ChatGPT Cluster
- First major difficulty spike
- Introduces boss battle music
- Tests player skills learned in Tier 1-2
- Weapon powerup available

### Boss: Level 10 - Webex Warfare
- Major difficulty increase
- Pure enemy type focus
- Health powerup for survival
- Speed increase to 9

### Boss: Level 11 - Teams Terror
- Penultimate challenge
- Shield powerup for defense
- Near-maximum difficulty
- Prepares for final boss

### Final Boss: Level 12 - Interview Inferno
- Ultimate challenge combining all elements
- Maximum speed and difficulty
- All enemy types present
- Epic conclusion to story

## Visual Consistency

All cutscene assets should maintain:
- **Futuristic/dystopian** aesthetic
- **Job market themes** (resumes, applications, meetings, etc.)
- **Transparent backgrounds** for proper layering
- **Consistent color palette** throughout levels
- **Progressive intensity** in visual effects as difficulty increases

## Audio Consistency

All audio narration should:
- Convey the **emotional weight** of job market struggles
- Build **tension** for boss encounters
- Celebrate **victories** in outro scenes
- Maintain **consistent voice** and tone
- Use **subtitle-friendly** pacing (5-10 seconds per scene)

---

This design ensures a cohesive, progressively challenging experience that tells a complete story while providing engaging gameplay!
